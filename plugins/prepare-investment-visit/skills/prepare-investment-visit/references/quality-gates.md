# Quality gates

Do not deliver until all applicable checks pass.

## Intake and identity

- The intake gate captured the meeting person, materials status, and podcast choice before research.
- The exact company, legal entities, and meeting person are resolved or ambiguity is disclosed.
- Same-name risks and unknown decision authority are disclosed.
- Supplied materials were read and cited by file plus page/slide, or an exact read failure was reported.

## Evidence and freshness

- Research cutoff appears near the beginning.
- Recent developments were searched separately.
- Decision-relevant numbers, customers, roles, financing events, and recent events have nearby citations.
- Supplied-material statements are labeled as company claims unless independently verified.
- Source grades, limitations, calculations, units, and metric definitions are present.
- Missing disclosure is not rewritten as proof of absence.

## Investment usefulness

- The first two pages state meeting objective, current action, thesis, key positives, key risks, and highest-value questions.
- The note separates market attractiveness, company quality, evidence quality, and investability.
- The meeting-person section is evidence-backed and materially changes the questions or meeting plan.
- Competitors include substitutes, incumbents, vertical integration, and failed analogues when relevant.
- At least one plausible bear case and hard gate is considered.
- Questions request concrete evidence and link to hypotheses or contradictions.

## PDF quality

- The required output is a real PDF, not a renamed Markdown file.
- All pages were rendered to images and visually inspected.
- Chinese glyphs, tables, URLs, page breaks, headers, footers, and page numbers are legible and unclipped.
- Citations are human-readable and links are clickable where supported.
- No empty pages, placeholder tokens, broken characters, overlapping text, or black squares remain.

## Podcast quality

- Podcast content is an adapted 5-10 minute narrative, not a verbatim note reading.
- It covers company, market, meeting person, fact boundaries, risks, and five key questions.
- The audio file exists, duration is checked, and no new facts were added.
- AI-generated voice disclosure is included.

## Confidentiality and tone

- Confidential materials are not exposed beyond the authorized task or quoted more than necessary.
- Language is direct, neutral, and free of unsupported promotional adjectives.
- The note states its evidence boundary and does not claim to be formal due diligence.
